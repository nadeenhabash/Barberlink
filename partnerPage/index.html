Questions? We've got answers

Why should I partner with BarberLink?

BarberLink helps barbershops grow their business, reach new customers, and increase brand awareness online. By partnering with us, you can get access to thousands of new customers who want to book appointments with barbershops like yours.

How do I become a BarberLink partner?

Simply fill out the registration form above with your barbershop details and we'll get in touch with you to complete the onboarding process.

Is BarberLink available in my country?

We are currently operating in Jordan. Check back soon for expansion to other countries.

Why do you charge commission fee?

Our commission fee covers the cost of marketing, platform maintenance, customer support, and payment processing to help grow your business.

What documents do I need to become a BarberLink partner?

You'll need a valid commercial license from the Ministry of Industry, Trade, and Supply, business registration documents, and bank account details for payments.

What is the radius of the BarberLink service?

Customers can book appointments from anywhere within your city. You set your own service area and availability.

How will I receive bookings from BarberLink?

You'll receive booking notifications via SMS/email. You can manage all appointments through the dashboard.

How frequently will I get paid?

Payments are processed weekly directly to your registered bank account after deducting the commission fee.

What happens once I go live?

Once approved, your barbershop will be visible to customers on the BarberLink platform. You'll start receiving bookings and can manage them through your partner dashboard.
